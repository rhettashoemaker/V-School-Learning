About Us

Flowers, like food, decorations, music, and special dress help us mark the special occasions in our lives. But they can be prohibitively expensive. Because of their diverse growing needs, the floral industry can carry a large carbon footprint. Floral materials often trade many sets of hands before landing in those of consumers, which process means many markups from point A to point Q. We had an idea that our love of flowers didn't have to come at such steep costs. In fact, if you look around at the carefully (or not so carefully) tended yards of in our town, our lovely town of Springville is a fully-stocked floral shop in itself.
